T'was the night before Christmas and on each campus floor
Not a student was stirring except Miss Lagore!
'Though the lab lonely this holiday sabbath,
Lagore stayed late working on something mammoth!
The blackboard obscured below layers of chalk.
Formulae fetched faculty and not poppycock!
Stepping back to detangle this detailed morass,
Scientifically, she had proven Christmas!
"Eureka!" she shouted! Lagore was in good cheer!
'Though she b'lieved herself solus, somebody was here...
In the shadows, a figure. "Bravo!" its voice shrieked.
"Afraid I must stop you, lest this festive find leak!"
"You'll stop me?" Lagore turned, now facing the foe.
"But Christmas is fact! The whole world—it must know!"
"Christmas?" it cussed, "Why, bah humbug's too good!"
"Blotting out the stat holiday's my livelihood..."
Miss Lagore was outraged—veins popped out her neck!
Lib'ral agendas, on her, have this effect!
The villain advanced, vehemence vexing all faith:
Academic apparition, Faux-Christmas Wraith!
Malign mist manifested as putrid breath breathed:
"Culture condemn you, priv'leged race, class and creed!"
